How you come in

You participate in Barakoa through a partnership with FABRi as a Fashion Curator.

This is about shaping how Barakoa lives on the body — through clothing, accessories, and styling.


Your work will be to work with to FABRi conceptualize, select, and present fashion in a compelling narrative, transforming it from mere clothing into an artistic experience that aligns with barakoa's message and theme.

What this looks like

As a Fashion Curator, you will collaborate closely with FABRi to bring the NiloFunk aesthetic to life through fashion.

Your contribution supports:

  • Translating Nilofunk into silhouettes and styling language
  • Developing the Barakoa fashion line and character costumes
  • Working with partner fashion houses on execution
  • Maintaining coherence across audience dress code and performers

How we’ll communicate

FABRi will share context monthly via email, supported by short voice notes for nuance.

Alignment continues through text or scheduled calls — paced, intentional, and respectful of your practice.

Who You’ll Work Closely With

Experience & Style Curator

You’ll align closely with the Experience & Style Curator to ensure that fashion, styling, and cultural atmosphere reinforce one another across the experience.

FABRi (Fabrication Practice)

FABRi anchors concept, material logic, and design direction. Your role adds craft, intuition, and fashion intelligence within that structure.

Partner Fashion Houses

Where execution is shared, you collaborate with partner houses doing hands-on production, helping maintain coherence across pieces.
