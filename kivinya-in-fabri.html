How you come in

You participate in Barakoa through a partnership with Fabri as an Experience & Style Curator.

This is about shaping the felt culture of the experience — how it looks, how it moves, and how people carry it with them before and after the gig.

What this looks like

This isn’t a rigid role or a heavy commitment. It’s a creative partnership rooted in intuition, taste, and cultural intelligence.

Your presence supports:

  • Shaping the overall aesthetic and emotional tone of Barakoa
  • Helping Fabri translate theme into lived experience
  • Connecting aligned creatives and cultural networks where useful
  • Supporting the Style Box logic without needing to be on-ground

How we’ll communicate

We keep things light, clear, and human.

Fabri shares context monthly via email, followed by a short voice note. Ongoing conversations can happen over text or scheduled calls, depending on what’s needed.

Who You’ll Work Closely With

Experience & Style Curator

You’ll align closely with the Experience & Style Curator to ensure that fashion, styling, and cultural atmosphere reinforce one another across the experience.

Fabri (Fabrication Practice)

Fabri anchors concept, material logic, and design direction. Your role adds craft, intuition, and fashion intelligence within that structure.

Partner Fashion Houses

Where execution is shared, you collaborate with partner houses doing hands-on production, helping maintain coherence across pieces.
