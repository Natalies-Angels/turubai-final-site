KEMBOI IN BARAKOA

event flow and breakdown

June 19
Onja Onja

Format: PANEL conversation, beer crafting, live music

Function: intimate, 'VIP', Early gig access

Total Duration: 3-4 hours

Exclusive Experience

On June 19th, Barakoa hosts an invite-only experience for a limited group of attendees who will also have full access to the main gig on June 20th.

This night is structured as a hybrid of:

  • a panel discussion, in partnership with Drunken Lectures
  • beer crafting and tasting
  • Live sound and performance
The Panel (Lecture) Theme — Ootzology

The lecture explores Ootzology — the ontology of oontz — looking at groove, repetition, rhythm, and how people gather around sound.

Your Role on June 19th

You will be present on ground with the band, DJs, sound engineer, vocalists, and indie artists.

During the panel and lecture, we would like a short live performance set of approximately 30 minutes, shared within the flow of conversation.

This is a live illustration of what we are talking about — rhythm, groove, voice, and presence.

Your contribution helps translate theory into feeling.

June 20
Evening Performance — Pre-Dunda / Ritual Session

Format: Live Performance

Function: Hype & Transition

Total Segment Duration: ~3 Hours

Your Set: ~2 Hours

On June 20th, we want you as a core vocal presence in a three-hour evening segment that ushers people from arrival into the party scene.

This segment sits between ritual and celebration.

Structure & Style
  • Your active performance time: approximately 2 hours
  • Intermissions and reset moments
  • Indie artist performances

This is a collaborative, fluid performance. You will perform your own songs, collaborate live with other vocalists, reinterpret familiar songs, hits, and bangers, and move between leading, responding, and sharing space.

Think Coke Studio–style energy — expansive, generous, and grounded.

Pre-June:

Function: Behind-the-scenes Building for the main gig

Sound & Curation

Ahead of June, with your permission, your music becomes part of the curation of the Barakoa beat.

This includes:

  • Referencing your existing songs
  • Integrating elements into the Nilofunktronica sound language
  • Clear credit and acknowledgment in the Barakoa Listening Book

Creatives’ Meet & Greet

In early May, Barakoa hosts an invite-only Creatives’ Meet & Greet edition. A gathering for the artists and collaborators behind Barakoa to meet in person, test what has been built so far, and align creatively ahead of June.

Your presence in this won't be a performance. It's more:

  • Mic checks
  • Sound testing
  • Light vocal runs if needed

This is fully dependent on sponsors, as we intend for it to be a fully funded experience, with all creatives compensated for their energy.

Logistics & Care

For all Barakoa engagements, food, transport, and accommodation will be provided. Styling for June performances will be done by FABRi, aligning fashion with the Barakoa world and Nilofunk theme.

Kemboi, we’re excited about the energy you bring on stage ... how you connect with people, how you move between seriousness and play, and how you wear your identity confidently. Barakoa needs that.
